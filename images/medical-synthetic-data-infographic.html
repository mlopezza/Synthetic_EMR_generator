Prompt Engineering Guide

Step-by-Step Structure to Build a
Complex Medical Synthetic Data Prompt

12 structured steps for generating realistic, complete Electronic Medical Records (EMR) with AI

🔵 Foundation & Role Setup
1 Step 1

Define the General Context

  • Explain what you are building
  • Clarify the purpose of the project
  • Specify that the data must be synthetic
  • Indicate how the data will be used
  • Define the required language
Structural Example "I am developing an app that…" · "I need synthetic EMR data to…" · "All content must be written in English."
2 Step 2

Clearly Define the LLM's Task

  • Explicitly state what the model must generate
  • Specify the document type (e.g., complete EMR)
  • Indicate required level of detail
  • Clarify it must simulate a real clinical document
  • State that Assessment & Plan sections are mandatory
Structural Example "The LLM must generate complete EMRs." · "The output must resemble real-world medical documentation."
3 Step 3

Assign a Professional Role

  • Specify the clinical perspective
  • Define the level of expertise
  • Set the tone and writing style (formal, technical)
Structural Example "Act as a Family Physician, Internal Medicine specialist, or Clinical Physician." · "Use professional medical terminology."
Family Physician Internal Medicine Clinical Physician Formal Tone Medical Terminology
Document Structure
📋 Step 4 — Define the Mandatory Document Structure

A · Cumulative Patient Profile (CPP)

  • Full identification
  • Active medical problems
  • Past medical & surgical history
  • Allergies and adverse reactions
  • Current medications
  • Immunization history
  • Family and social history

B · Encounter Notes (SOAP)

  • Chief Complaint
  • History of Present Illness
  • Physical Examination (incl. vital signs)
  • Clinical Impression / Diagnosis
  • Management Plan
SOAP

C · Complementary Documentation

  • Laboratory reports
  • Imaging reports
  • Consultation notes
  • Hospital discharge summaries
  • Informed consent
  • Communication logs
  • Referral letters
Deliverables & Input
5 Step 5

Specify Number & Format of Deliverables

  • Total number of patients
  • One document per patient
  • All compiled into individual PDF files
  • Each PDF must contain the complete record
6 Step 6

Provide Base Clinical Input for Each Patient

  • Demographics & relevant history
  • Presenting complaint
  • Key clinical findings
  • Required diagnostic tests & prescriptions
  • Referrals if needed
  • Hospitalization requirement (≥1 case)
  • Discharge summary if hospitalized
  • Medical leave documentation if applicable
Data Rules & Specifications
⚙️ Technical Specifications
7 Step 7

Synthetic Data Generation Rules

  • Fill missing info realistically
  • Maintain internal clinical consistency
  • Only alter lab values specified as abnormal
  • Populate unspecified values within normal ranges
  • Simulate realistic imaging narratives
  • Simulate realistic referral letters
8 Step 8

Prescription Requirements

  • Generic drug name
  • Presentation (tablet, vial, etc.)
  • Dosage
  • Frequency
  • Quantity
  • Duration
  • Route of administration
9 Step 9

Laboratory Report Requirements

  • Include all parameters for each test
  • Provide units
  • Include reference ranges
  • Include both normal & abnormal values
  • Only abnormal values = those provided in case
10 Step 10

Imaging Report Requirements

  • Study type and technique
  • Normal findings
  • Abnormal findings (as specified)
  • Detailed radiological description
  • Final impression
11 Step 11

Special Clinical Instructions

  • At least one hospitalization case
  • Include ICU/intermediate care details if specified
  • Include treatment course
  • Include discharge plan
  • Include follow-up orders
  • Include medical leave if required
12 Step 12

Realism & Quality Standards

  • Must resemble real-world EMR documentation
  • Use professional clinical language
  • Maintain logical medical reasoning
  • Do not omit Assessment & Plan
  • Ensure consistency across all documents
  • Make reports look authentic
